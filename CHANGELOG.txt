NOTE ON HUNT ENGINE:
V16 replaces the v15 angle-based parallax hunt with a true Wolf3D raycaster.
The DDA (Digital Differential Analyzer) algorithm casts one ray per screen column,
computing wall hit distance for accurate perspective projection. Enemy sprites use
camera-plane inverse-transform for correct screen positioning and are z-buffered
so they cannot clip through walls. Forward/back movement with collision detection.

NOTE ON TRAVEL ANIMATION:
Biomes are determined by stop index:
  stops 0-2: desert (Las Vegas / Phoenix / El Paso)
  stops 3-5: jungle (Mexico City / Oaxaca / Guatemala City)
  stops 6-7: coast (Managua / San Jose / Panama City)
  stops 8-9: mountain (Medellin approach, Colombia highlands)
  stop 10:   city (Bogota)
